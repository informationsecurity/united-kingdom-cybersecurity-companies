- Credential theft and personal data
- Account takeover (ATO)
- Financial fraud
- Supply chain attacks (targeted phishing on suppliers)
- Fake portal simulations (login, banking, ecommerce)

In this guide you will find the **10 best UK providers for phishing and smishing protection in 2025**, with focus on detection, takedown, simulations and training.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### What is Phishing & Smishing?
Phishing and smishing are social engineering techniques used to deceive people and obtain sensitive information such as passwords and financial data. Phishing occurs through fraudulent emails, while smishing uses text messages.

### When and why is it necessary?
It is necessary to protect against phishing and smishing to prevent fraud, identity theft and financial damage. With the increase in cyber threats, proactive protection is essential to ensure data security.

### What is the average cost?
The average cost for protection against phishing and smishing varies based on the complexity of solutions and specific company needs. It is important to evaluate the quality-price ratio and scalability of offered solutions.

### How do you choose the right supplier?
To choose the right supplier, it is important to evaluate technical skills, certifications, adopted methodologies, support offered and partner reputation. Also consider the target clientele type and solution flexibility.

### What are the important certifications?
Important certifications include ISO 27001, GDPR, and other recognized certifications in the cybersecurity sector. These guarantee a rigorous approach compliant with data protection regulations.
